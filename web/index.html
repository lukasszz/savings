Savings

Manage your financial goals across multiple assets

How this app helps you to manage your savings?

Split your incomes into budgets

Load money to one of your bank accounts and split it across your budgets. Money on your assets and budgets will be tracked separately but coherently.

Transfer money among budgets

Transfer your money to another budget without changing your asset balance.

History

Browse the history of all your transactions in the context of the selected account. or budget.
